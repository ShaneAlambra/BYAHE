Partners in life, partners on the road

It all started in January 2025 when we brought home our Honda ADV 160. From short trips around the neighborhood to before-sunrise departures heading north — the rides kept getting longer, and so did the list of places we've been.

For us, it was never just about the destination. It's the roadside coffee stops, the mountain wind, and having the person you love riding behind you through every long stretch of highway. These are our rides.
